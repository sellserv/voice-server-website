Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 15, 2026

Privacy Overview

SellServ Voice is a self-hosted communication platform. This policy describes the data handling practices for the official SellServ-hosted instance (chat.sellserv.net).

Self-Hosting & Desktop App

If you self-host SellServ Voice on your own infrastructure or use the desktop app to connect to a self-hosted server, this policy does not apply as you (or your server administrator) control 100% of your data. The desktop application itself does not collect, transmit, or store any telemetry or personal data to SellServ.

Data We Collect

Account Information

When you create an account, we store your username, display name, email address (for recovery), and any profile media you upload. Passwords are cryptographically hashed.

Messages and Content

We store the text messages, files, and media you send in channels. This data is only accessible to members of those specific servers or conversations.

Voice and Video

Audio and video streams are transmitted in real-time via WebRTC. These streams flow through our media server for relay only and are never recorded or stored.

Technical Data

We use secure session tokens (JWT) and WebSocket connection states. We do not use third-party analytics trackers or advertising SDKs.

Data Usage & Sharing

Your data is used solely to operate the communication service. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties. Data is only visible to the intended recipients within the platform's permission system.

Security & Retention

We use industry-standard encryption (TLS/HTTPS) for all traffic. Passwords are hashed using bcrypt. Your data is retained as long as your account is active; deleting your account removes your data from our systems.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at support@sellserv.net.

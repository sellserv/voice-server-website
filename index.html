A complete platform for your community, self-hosted and open-source.

Voice Channels

Real-time voice communication powered by WebRTC and mediasoup SFU. Low latency, high quality, multiple channels.

💬

Text Chat

Full-featured messaging with replies, pins, reactions, mentions, file uploads, and link previews.

Roles & Permissions

Multi-role system with per-channel and per-group permission overrides. Fine-grained access control.

DMs & Calls

Private direct messages and one-on-one voice/video calls between users.

Custom Emoji & Soundboard

Upload custom emoji for reactions and messages. Play sound effects in voice channels via the soundboard.

Bot System

Build and integrate bots to automate tasks, moderate content, or add fun commands.

Watch Parties

Watch videos together in sync with your community. Shared playback controls for everyone.

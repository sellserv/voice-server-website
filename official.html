How to Join

1

Connect

Open chat.sellserv.net in your browser or enter the URL in the desktop app's server field.

2

Create an Account

Sign up with a username, email, and password. You'll get a verification email to activate your account.

3

Create a Server

Once logged in, click the + button to create your own server. Set a name, invite friends, and start chatting.

Free During Alpha

SellServ Voice is currently in Alpha, so all Pro features are completely free for everyone on the official instance. Once the Alpha period ends, Pro will become a paid plan at $5/month to help support ongoing development and server costs.

Self-Hosting?

If you run your own instance, you have access to all features for free — Alpha or not. The Pro plan only applies to the official public instance at chat.sellserv.net.

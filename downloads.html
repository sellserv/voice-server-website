Mobile

📱

iOS (iPhone / iPad)

Use the web app directly from your home screen — no App Store download required.

  1. Open your server URL in Safari
  2. Tap the Share button (square with arrow)
  3. Scroll down and tap Add to Home Screen
  4. Tap Add — the app icon appears on your home screen
🤖

Android

Install the web app to your home screen — no Play Store download required.

  1. Open your server URL in Chrome
  2. Tap the three-dot menu (top right)
  3. Tap Add to Home screen or Install app
  4. Tap Add — the app icon appears on your home screen

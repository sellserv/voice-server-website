Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 15, 2026

Overview

SellServ Voice is a self-hosted communication platform. When you use the official SellServ-hosted instance or the SellServ Voice desktop app, this policy describes what data we handle and how.

If you self-host SellServ Voice on your own infrastructure, you control all data. This policy applies only to the SellServ-operated service.

Data We Collect

Account Information

When you create an account, we store:

  • Username and display name
  • Password (hashed with bcrypt — we never store or see your plain-text password)
  • Email address (optional, only if you provide one for account recovery)
  • Profile avatar and banner (if uploaded)

Messages and Content

  • Text messages you send in channels and direct messages
  • Files, images, and custom emoji you upload
  • Poll votes and reactions

Voice and Video

  • Audio and video streams are transmitted in real-time via WebRTC and are not recorded or stored
  • Voice and video data flows through our media server (mediasoup SFU) for relay only — no recordings are made

Technical Data

  • Authentication tokens (JWT) stored in browser cookies for session management
  • WebSocket connection state for real-time features
  • We do not use analytics trackers, advertising SDKs, or third-party data collection tools

How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to operate the communication service:

  • Authenticate your identity and maintain your session
  • Deliver messages, files, and media to intended recipients
  • Display your profile to other users in servers you belong to
  • Enforce server moderation (automod, bans, role permissions)

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties.

Your messages and content are visible only to other members of the servers and direct message conversations you participate in, subject to role permissions set by server administrators.

Data Storage and Security

  • All data is stored on our server infrastructure
  • Passwords are hashed using bcrypt
  • Authentication uses signed JWT tokens with CSRF protection
  • WebSocket and API connections use TLS encryption (HTTPS/WSS)
  • Optional two-factor authentication (TOTP) is available for account security

Data Retention

Your account data and messages are retained as long as your account exists. If you delete your account, your data is removed from our systems. Server administrators can delete messages and channels within their servers.

Your Rights

You can:

  • Update or delete your profile information at any time
  • Delete your messages
  • Leave servers to stop sharing data with those communities
  • Request account deletion by contacting us

Children's Privacy

SellServ Voice is not intended for use by anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at support@sellserv.net.
